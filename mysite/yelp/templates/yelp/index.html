Clean Structure

The front end is built on bootstrap v3 and the backend is powered by Django framework. Standalone webserver Njinx powered the website.

Adequate Functionality

Sprout is a powerful website, which makes you easily find your favorite restaurant near you according to the rating and others' comments.

Intelligence

Powerful recommendation system using artificial intelligence make things even easier. We know what you like and give you the right recommendations.

User Centered

Get tired of various advertisements on other webistes? These pain has become the past! Our website is ads-free just for better user experience.

Powerful Database

MariaDB, fork of MySQL, running behind the webiste. Queries can be returnned fast and accurate! Currently, the database is from Yelp and our recoreds is increasing!

Chat Now

Sprout allows you connect with other user online! You now could share your ideas in real time! Invite your friends to join your dinner tonight online!
